Director's Message

A Message from Our Director

"At Nav Chetana Public School, we believe the primary years shape a child's confidence, habits, and love for learning. Our mission is to give every student a disciplined, caring, and motivating environment where the basics are taught with patience."

Together with our teachers and parents, we focus on reading, writing, mathematics, communication, personal discipline, and moral values so that children are ready for the next stage of education with clarity and self-belief.

About Our School

A Strong Start for Every Child

Nav Chetana Public School is dedicated to building a clear academic foundation and positive habits in young learners. Learning that is personal, practical, and child-friendly.

We currently educate students up to Class 5, giving each child the attention, structure, and encouragement needed in the early school years. Our approach balances classroom learning with activities, discipline, cleanliness, communication skills, and respect for parents, teachers, and community.
